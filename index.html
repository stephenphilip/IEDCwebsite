About RSET IEDC

Be a part of the most funded club at RSET

At RSET IEDC, we foster the spirit of innovation and entrepreneurship right here on our campus at Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology. Our Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centers (IEDCs) serve as dynamic incubators, igniting the entrepreneurial flame within students from all disciplines. We are proud to have the backing of Kerala Startup Mission, which supports 283 IEDCs across the state. Come and be a part of the most funded club on the campus
